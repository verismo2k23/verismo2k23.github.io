Whiz Holics

Registration Fee : 200 ( Team of 2 )
First Prize : 3000
Second Prize : 1500

The master mind is a man/woman with a plan to outwit complexity. If you are a master mind then come and kick the show.

The event comprises of 3 different competitions

  • Mind Crusade : Quiz
  • Spellspire : Word Hunt
  • Scavenger : Problem Solving

Whiz Holics Rules and Regulations
								
1. Three events will be held in three different rounds.
	-> Mind Crusade(Quiz)
	-> Spellspire(Word Hunt)
	-> Scavenger (Problem Solving)
2. Every team has to participate in all three rounds.
3. The team with the most cumulative points from all rounds will be declared as the winner.
4. There will be a strict time limit for each event.
5. The team must assemble at 10 am itself.
6. College ID card is mandatory for participation.
7. Larceny and vandalism of any property in the venue can lead to disciplinary actions.
8. Maintaining discipline is obligatory throughout the contest.
9. A teammate having a mobile phone with a good internet connection is preferred for buzzer purposes.
10. You can bring water and medications if needed.
11. Organizers reserve every right to add or remove any rules as they fit.

Buzzer Rules

Since this game is revolving around the buzzer there is some set of rules on the buzzer system.

1. In each stage, a team gets only one chance to press the buzzer.
2. Only the buzzer-pressed teams are considered for evaluation.
3. After the allotted time, the host/volunteers check the answer so wait until the timer ends.
4. If a team presses the buzzer, it’s illegal to change the answer and can lead to disqualification.
5. If a team presses the buzzer and the answer is wrong/takes a break in answering, they won’t get any points as well as another chance to change it and they need to wait until that stage ends. I.e., they can only participate in upcoming levels.
6. If say two teams press the buzzer at the same time and the answers are also correct then the points get distributed equally as a whole.
7. If a team presses the buzzer after the allotted time, then the answer will not be considered even though it is correct.
