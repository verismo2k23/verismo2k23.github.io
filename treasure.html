Elemental Quest

Registration Fee : 300 ( Team of 4 )
First Prize : 3500
Second Prize : 1500

Its the time to test your navigational, memory and knowledge skills at Campus. We are conducting a Treasure Hunt, that will help you refresh your memory skills to Lecture Venues, or orientate yourself with new venues.

Treasure Hunt Rules and Regulations

1.	Time for the hunt is 2 hrs.
2.	Each team should have only 4 members.
3.	Team numbers will be given to every participating team.
4.	A clue will be provided to the participants they have to solve it and find the next clue and go on . The clues will either be hidden or visible.
5.	Every clue card or clue will have the number of their team on it, so the team should pick the clues assigned to them.
6.	If the participants is not able to find the last clue , then the team who found the previous clue first will be announced as the winner.
7.	Participating team are not allowed to enter classrooms and they are not allowed to use phone if found they will be disqualified.
8.	A team has to submit all the collected clues to be rewarded as winner at the last stage.
